# Launch Planning Workflow

Coordinate all aspects of a product launch from planning to post-launch review.

## When to Use

- New product or feature launches
- Major version releases
- Market entry launches
- Any task with category: `launch` or `gtm`

## Inputs Required

- **Launch name**: What are we launching?
- **Launch date**: When is the launch?
- **Launch type**: Product launch, feature launch, market entry, etc.

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Gather Context

Read relevant files:
- `Knowledge/product-context.md` - Product and audience info
- `GOALS.md` - Strategic alignment
- Any existing launch plans in `Knowledge/launch-plans/`

### Step 2: Create Launch Checklist

Create a comprehensive launch checklist task with sections:

- [ ] Product announcement content
- [ ] Sales enablement materials
- [ ] Marketing campaign assets
- [ ] Developer documentation
- [ ] Customer communications
- [ ] Competitive positioning
- [ ] Launch metrics tracking setup
- [ ] Post-launch review scheduled

### Step 3: Identify Dependencies

Flag tasks that depend on:
- Product readiness
- Sales team availability
- Marketing calendar
- Engineering documentation

### Step 4: Set Timeline

Break down launch tasks by week:
- **4 weeks out**: Planning and strategy
- **3 weeks out**: Content creation begins
- **2 weeks out**: Sales enablement ready
- **1 week out**: Final review and approvals
- **Launch week**: Execution and monitoring
- **Post-launch**: Review and optimization

## Success Criteria

- [ ] All launch components identified
- [ ] Dependencies mapped
- [ ] Timeline established
- [ ] Cross-functional alignment confirmed
- [ ] Metrics tracking planned
