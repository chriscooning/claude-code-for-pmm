# Sales Enablement Workflow

Create effective sales materials that help sales teams win deals.

## When to Use

- Creating battle cards
- Building sales decks
- Writing objection handling guides
- Any task with category: `sales-enablement`

## Inputs Required

- **Material type**: Battle card, sales deck, objection guide, etc.
- **Target audience**: Enterprise, SMB, specific persona
- **Use case**: Competitive win, feature highlight, objection handling

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1: Gather Context

Read:
- `Knowledge/product-context.md` - Product and competitive info
- `Knowledge/competitive-intel/` - Competitive positioning
- `Knowledge/customer-stories/` - Customer success stories
- `GOALS.md` - Strategic priorities

### Step 2: Identify Key Messages

Extract:
- Value propositions
- Differentiation points
- Win themes
- Common objections

### Step 3: Create Material Structure

**For Battle Cards:**
- Competitor overview
- Our differentiation
- Key talking points
- Win strategy
- Objection handling

**For Sales Decks:**
- Problem statement
- Solution overview
- Key features/benefits
- Proof points (case studies)
- Call to action

**For Objection Guides:**
- Common objection
- Root cause
- Response framework
- Supporting evidence
- Next steps

### Step 4: Review and Refine

Ensure:
- Clear, actionable language
- Sales-friendly format
- Evidence-backed claims
- Aligned with product positioning

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Material addresses specific sales need
- [ ] Clear differentiation vs competitors
- [ ] Actionable for sales team
- [ ] Aligned with product messaging
- [ ] Includes proof points or evidence
